OUR DEPARTMENT

The Department of Mechanical Engineering aims to produce the Engineer's with the abilities.

  • To design and conduct experiments,as well as to analyze and interpret data.
  • To design a system,component to meet desired needs.
  • To function on multi-disciplinary team.
  • To identify,formulates,ans solves engineering problems.
  • Understanding of professional and ethical responsibility.
  • To use the technique,skills,and modern engineering tools which are neccesary for engineering practice,recognition of the need for,and an ability to engage in lifelong learning.

    • VISION

    Centre of Outstanding Education and Research in the field of Mechanical Engineering.

    MISSION

    • Impart highest quality education to the students to build their capacity and innovative skills to the solve the industrial needs and social issues.
    • Develop alliances with Research and Development,Indrustrial Organization and alumni for excellence in the Consultancy and product design.
    • Enhancing the knowledge and expertise through the professional programme in thermal,manufacturing and industrial Engineering.
